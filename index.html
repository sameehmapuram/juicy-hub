Our Story

Founded in 2015, FreshSqueeze was born out of a passion for health and wellness. Our mission is simple: to provide delicious, nutrient-packed juices made from the freshest ingredients.

We source our fruits and vegetables from local organic farms whenever possible, ensuring you get the highest quality products with maximum nutritional benefits.

All our juices are cold-pressed to preserve enzymes and nutrients, and we never add sugar or preservatives. What you see is what you get - pure, natural goodness in every bottle.
